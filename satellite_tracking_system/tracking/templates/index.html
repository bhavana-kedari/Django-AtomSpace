Welcome to the Satellite Tracking System!

Embark on a cosmic journey with our Django-powered satellite tracking application. Explore the mysteries of space as you navigate through a galaxy of satellite data and launch country information.

Our system orbits around two main celestial bodies:

Satellites: this table holds key information such as their names, NORAD IDs, orbits, and even their launch countries.

Launch Countries: This table specifies where our satellites take their first leap into space, each with its own unique name and launch code.

All satellite data has been meticulously gathered from NORAD's database, while launch countries were spontaneously generated.
